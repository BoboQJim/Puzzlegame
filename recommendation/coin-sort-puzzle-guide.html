Coin Sort Puzzle Strategy: Master the 1-to-15 Infinite Loop

Published: 22 January 2026 Category: Logic / Merge

Coin Sort Puzzle is a deceptively simple yet highly addictive numeric logic game that has taken the casual gaming world by storm. It combines the satisfying mechanics of sorting with the strategic depth of number merging. Your goal is simple: stack identical coins to merge them into higher values. But here's the twist—once you hit 15, the cycle resets to 1, creating an endless loop of potential if you can manage your board space effectively.

Phase 1: Understanding the Merge Mechanics

The game operates on a 5-slot grid. When you place coins of the same number into a slot, they merge. For example, stacking ten "1" coins might merge into a single "2" coin. This mechanic is crucial for clearing space.

Key Rule: Unlike 2048, you aren't just doubling numbers. You are progressing linearly from 1 to 15. The magic happens when you merge 15s—they generate new "1" coins, allowing the game to continue indefinitely. This circular progression is what makes Coin Sort Puzzle unique.

Phase 2: Speed Sorting & Board Management

As you progress, the game speeds up. You'll be dealt a hand of mixed coins that you must distribute into your slots. Here is where most players fail: Greed.

  • Don't Rush the shuffle: You have the option to deal new cards or shuffle. Only use this when you absolutely have no moves left.
  • Keep One Slot Open: Try to keep at least one of your 5 slots empty or nearly empty. This "buffer zone" is essential for temporarily holding a coin that doesn't fit anywhere else while you set up a merge in another slot.
  • Group by Range: Try to keep low numbers (1-5) on the left and high numbers (10-15) on the right. This visual organization helps your brain react faster during speed rounds.

Phase 3: Reaching the "15" Milestone

Reaching the number 15 is the ultimate test of patience. The coins will pile up, and space will become scarce. At this stage, every move must result in a merge.

Pro Tip: If you have a stack of 13s and 14s, prioritize them above all else. Clearing them triggers the reset, which often wipes out a large portion of the board, giving you a fresh start with a high score boost.

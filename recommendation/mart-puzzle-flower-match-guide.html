Mart Puzzle Flower Match Strategy Guide: Master the Petal Sorting

Published: 04 February 2026 Category: Logic / Sorting

Mart Puzzle Flower Match is a relaxing yet challenging puzzle game where you play as a floral organizer. The concept is simple: collect 3 matching flowers to clear a flower pot and move on to the next. However, the game adds a layer of complexity with its 3D stacking mechanics. Flowers are buried under piles, and you must strategically move them to your limited "holding slots" to reach the ones you need. It's a test of foresight and efficient sorting.

Phase 1: The Core Loop (Collect & Clear)

At the top of the screen, you'll see the target flower pots indicating which flowers are currently in demand. Your goal is to find these specific flowers in the pile below.

The Matching Rule: Tapping a flower moves it to your holding bar at the bottom. Once you have collected 3 identical flowers in the bar (e.g., 3 Red Roses), they are automatically bundled, cleared, and added to the target pot. The pot then updates to a new demand, keeping the game flowing.

Phase 2: Managing the Layers

The real challenge lies in the "buried" flowers. You can't just tap any flower you see; many are blocked by others on top. You often need to move "blocker" flowers into your holding bar to access the ones you need.

  • Watch Your Slots: Your holding bar has limited space (usually 7 slots). If you fill it up with non-matching flowers, it's Game Over.
  • Strategic Holding: Only move a non-target flower to the holding bar if it reveals a match or clears a path to a vital piece. Don't click randomly!
  • The Rule of Three: Always prioritize completing a set of 3. Clearing a set frees up 3 slots in your holding bar, giving you breathing room to maneuver.

Gameplay Walkthrough: Floral Challenge

Watch this gameplay video to see how to handle a dense pile of flowers. Notice how we sometimes pick up a "useless" flower just to uncover the third match for a set we are already holding.

Phase 3: Using Power-Ups

When the pile gets too messy and your slots are full, use the in-game tools to save the day:

  • Clear Slot (Undo/Remove): Clears flowers from your holding bar back to the pile or removes them entirely. Essential when you are one click away from losing.
  • Add Pot: Adds an extra target pot at the top, allowing you to collect and clear more types of flowers simultaneously.
  • Expand Bar: Adds an extra slot to your holding bar, giving you more margin for error.
  • Refresh (Shuffle): Rearranges the flowers in the pile, potentially bringing buried matches to the surface.

Water Color Sort Guide: Mastering Liquid Puzzles with 3-Star Efficiency

Published: 8 January 2026 Category: Logic Puzzle

Water Color Sort is a captivating and mentally refreshing puzzle game that tests your logic and organization skills. The goal is to sort the colored water in the glasses until all colors are in the same glass. While it starts simple, the advanced levels require strategic planning and precise moves to achieve a perfect 3-star rating.

Core Mechanics: How to Play Water Color Sort

The rules are intuitive: you can only pour water into another tube if it is linked to the same color and there's enough space in the glass tube. Every move counts towards your final efficiency score.

Expert Strategies for 3-Star Rewards

To dominate the leaderboard and clear the board without getting stuck, consider these pro strategies:

  • Analyze the Tubes: Before your first move, look at the bottom layers. Identifying which colors are buried deepest helps you prioritize your pours.
  • Prioritize Empty Tubes: An empty tube is your greatest asset. Use it as a temporary "buffer" only when absolutely necessary to unlock a trapped color.
  • Top-Layer Management: Try to clear the colors that appear most frequently on the top layers first to create more movement space.
  • Think 3 Steps Ahead: Avoid "panic pouring." Ensure that every move doesn't block a future necessary sequence.
